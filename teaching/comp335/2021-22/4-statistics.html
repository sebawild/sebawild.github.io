Unit 4: Empirical Science & Statistics

This is an archived version of this module from the academic year 2021-22.
 Click here for the current iteration.

This unit covers:

  • basics of the scientific method
  • statistical tools for educational research

Learning outcomes

  1. Be able to competently use the terminology of empirical science, such as theory, hypothesis, experiment, null hypothesis.
  2. Be able to understand, explain, and evaluate empirical science research in the domain of education research.
  3. Be able to qualitatively explain the purpose and use of statistical hypothesis tests.

Pre-session task

Watch the Veritasium video below on the Replication Crisis of Science and answer these guiding questions on on Slido.
