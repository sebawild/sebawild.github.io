School Outreach

I am coordinating the school outreach activities of my department.
Below are some past projects and events; feel free to contact me to discuss what we do!

AI Summer School

We have developed an interactive online lecture on binary classification: Artificial Intelligence – How deep neural network learn introduces core notions of machine learning in a playful and engaging way. Students develop an intuitive understanding of binary classification tasks and how artificial neural networks are employed in solving these.

A core goal of the workshop is to help demystify artificial intelligence. While artificial-intelligence methods have found widespread adoption and have increasing influence on individuals and society at large, large parts of the general public have had little exposure to its fundamental working principles and limitations.
